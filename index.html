About Us

The marvels and spark of the team happened when a handful of normal students came together with a marvellous idea, that was solely based on their common interest, this was the sounds of the roaring engines to the speeding formula cars, burning their wheels on the asphalt. Later they carried out operations to help the team grow and to recruit the dedicated ones, that included exams and interviews. Hence, ZENITH was born.

MISSION

To have the lead and to inspire the Formula prototypes using economic and state-of-the-art technologies, all from scratch, and to compete in the Formula Bharat and FFS.

VISION

Seeking into the deepest of dedications and skillset, inspiring the nation to get into the art and the field of Formula student racing, and to craft engineers efficiently using efficient and budget friendly technologies.

College of Engineering Trivandrum

Founded in 1939 during the reign of the Travancore king, Sreechithira Thirunal Balarama Varma, College of Engineering Trivandrum commonly known as CET is the first engineering college in Kerala. Since inception, CET continues to maintain its excellence in technical education and research in the state. CET has been ranked 47th in Outlook magazine's list of top 100 engineering colleges in India 2016. Till date, the college has produced lakhs of graduates and under graduates whose alumni association of CETAA has firm grips all over the globe. As per the present statistics till date, CET tops the technical education in Kerala
